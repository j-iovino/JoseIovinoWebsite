    About

    I am a professor at the Department of Mathematics of the University of Texas at San Antonio.

    My research is in mathematical logic. My field within logic is model theory, which is the meta-mathematical study of the notion of mathematical truth.

    My current work includes the use of model-theoretic methods in quantum information, as well as the study of complexity and learnability of information in large networks and large data. This work is an ongoing collaboration with my colleagues Samson Alva and Eduardo Dueñez at the University of Texas at San Antonio.
